# In-App Content Disclosure

Any in-app content that is informational, promotional, or monetized should be disclosed accurately so users understand the source and purpose of what they are seeing. In Master QR, that includes consent messaging, help text, support guidance, privacy notices, ad placements, and any sponsored or partner-driven surface. The application should not blur the line between system functionality and commercial messaging. If content changes based on region, store flavor, or consent status, that variation should be documented and, where relevant, summarized in the in-app legal pages. This policy supports user trust by ensuring that the app’s visible content is not misleading, hidden, or editorially conflated with the core scanning and generation experience.
