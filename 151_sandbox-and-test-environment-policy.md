# Sandbox and Test Environment Policy

Development and test environments should be isolated from production credentials, production data, and release channels. Master QR may include sandbox variants for ad testing, consent evaluation, or internal QA, but those environments must be clearly labeled and restricted. The policy requires that dummy credentials, test ad IDs, and mock services never be accidentally shipped to users as production endpoints unless the release deliberately intends that configuration. Test data should be synthetic wherever practical, and any real data used for debugging must be approved and redacted. This document helps prevent accidental exposure of development artifacts while preserving the ability to validate privacy, monetization, and scanning behavior before public distribution.
