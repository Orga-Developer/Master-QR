# Analytics and Tracking Disclosure

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This document explains the purpose, scope, and controls associated with analytics instrumentation and tracking technologies used by the application. It should state what is measured, why it is measured, and whether identifiers or event data are linked to a device, session, or account.

## Scope
It applies to event logging, usage metrics, crash analysis, A/B testing, attribution systems, and any storage or transmission of analytics identifiers. The disclosure must also cover the extent to which tracking is performed by third parties rather than by the app operator itself.

## Operational Commitments
Users should be told whether tracking is essential, configurable, or entirely optional. Where consent is required, the document should explain how the opt-in or opt-out state is captured and where users can later revise that choice. The language should remain understandable to non-specialists while still being technically precise.

## Application to Master QR
For Master QR, analytics should be described as ancillary to the QR workflow and not as a prerequisite to scanning or code generation. If events are used to improve stability, diagnose failures, or understand feature adoption, those purposes should be articulated without overstating the sensitivity of the data.

## Review and Governance
The disclosure should be reconciled with the privacy policy, consent flow, and platform labels. Any analytics SDK added later must be matched against this document before public release.
