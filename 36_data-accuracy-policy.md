# Data Accuracy Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy describes how the organization ensures that stored data is correct, current, and fit for the purpose for which it is used. Its objective is to reduce harm caused by stale, contradictory, or misleading records.

## Scope
It applies to user details, support notes, consent states, analytics labels, localization strings, legal contact records, and any operational metadata that influences product behavior or compliance. The policy should specify how inaccuracies are corrected, how source truth is established, and how conflicts are resolved.

## Operational Commitments
Accuracy is not just a legal concept; it is also an operational control. If the app stores incorrect tags, outdated support contacts, or wrong consent states, the user experience and compliance posture both degrade. Therefore, accuracy checks should be built into routine maintenance rather than handled only after complaints.

## Application to Master QR
Master QR’s local history tagging and language settings should remain editable so users can correct inaccurate or stale values. Any generated notice or store-facing text should also be kept synchronized with the latest build and legal posture.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed when user-editable data models, localization files, or compliance notices change. A correction workflow should exist for both machine-managed and user-managed records.
