# Identifiable Data Minimization Policy

Master QR should collect the minimum amount of identifiable information required to operate its declared features. For a QR reader and generator, that principle typically means avoiding account creation, unnecessary profile data, and broad background telemetry. Where identifiers are unavoidable, they must be scoped to a legitimate operational purpose and reviewed for retention necessity. The application should avoid instrumenting analytics fields merely because they are technically available. This policy also applies to support workflows and legal notices, which should request only the details needed to resolve a problem or satisfy a request. Data minimization is both a privacy safeguard and a practical compliance strategy because it limits the impact of any future incident or misuse.
