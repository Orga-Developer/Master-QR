# Telemetry Opt-Out Policy

Users should have a clear path to limit or decline optional telemetry where the application or its partners collect usage or diagnostic data beyond what is strictly necessary for operation. Master QR may expose this choice through consent screens, platform settings, or a dedicated in-app privacy menu. The opt-out mechanism must be meaningful, not decorative; it should actually suppress the relevant data stream in subsequent sessions to the extent technically feasible. The policy should distinguish between telemetry that is essential to prevent abuse or maintain security and telemetry that supports analytics, optimization, or marketing. By making opt-out realistic and explainable, the app strengthens trust and reduces the risk of privacy misrepresentation.
