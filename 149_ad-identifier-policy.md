# Ad Identifier Policy

The ad identifier policy governs the use of advertising IDs and similar signals that may be employed by monetization or measurement partners. Master QR should clearly disclose whether the ad identifier is used for frequency capping, attribution, personalization, fraud detection, or analytics. The policy must also explain that the identifier can typically be reset or limited through operating-system privacy controls, and that consent choices may affect whether personalized advertising is available. Importantly, the app should not tie ad identifiers to highly sensitive user data or use them in ways inconsistent with platform policies. This document is central to ad transparency and should be kept aligned with the actual SDK configuration in each distributed variant.
