# Advertising, Personalized Advertising, and Monetization Notice

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This notice describes how advertising is presented, how ad impressions or clicks may be measured, and whether ad personalization is enabled. Its purpose is to let users understand the economic model of the app without hiding the trade-offs associated with ad-supported software. It also explains how ad-supported access contributes to the app’s monetization model and what that means for users.

## Scope
It applies to banner, interstitial, rewarded, native, or contextual placements, as well as to the reporting and frequency-capping logic that surrounds them. The notice should also state whether advertisers receive device identifiers, rough audience segments, or consent signals that affect ad selection. The notice should cover ad-funded access, feature sponsorship, and any revenue-sharing arrangement that may later be added to the product.

## Operational Commitments
Personalization language must be precise. If the app or its SDKs can serve non-personalized ads, that option should be disclosed; if consent is required in some regions, the notice should explain that the ad experience may change based on that choice. Avoid promising ad-free conditions that the build does not actually support. Where monetization affects a feature or screen, the document should say so plainly and should not bury commercial disclosures in footnotes or secondary pages.

## Application to Master QR
Master QR contains AdMob integration points and therefore needs a public statement that ads may be displayed in exchange for free access to the app. The notice should also make clear whether ad personalization influences only the ad slot or any surrounding recommendation surface. For Master QR, the notice should explicitly connect the ad experience to the app’s free-use model and should clarify whether any future premium tier would change the user journey.

## Review and Governance
The notice should be updated whenever a new ad network, mediation layer, or auction mechanism is added. Regional privacy rules should be checked before enabling any new audience targeting flow. The notice should be updated before any commercial change, especially if the app adds subscriptions, partner placements, or promoted content.
