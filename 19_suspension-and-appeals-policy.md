## Scope
It applies to account states, content moderation, billing disputes, security incidents, fraud signals, platform enforcement, and repeated policy violations. The document should avoid arbitrary discretion by identifying the general categories of conduct that may trigger enforcement action.

## Operational Commitments
Where an appeal path exists, the policy should explain what information the user should provide, how the appeal is reviewed, and whether any temporary restrictions remain in place during review. The process should be fair, timely, and proportionate to the risk involved.
