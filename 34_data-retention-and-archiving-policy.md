# Data Retention and Archiving Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy explains how long each category of data is kept, when it is archived, and when it is deleted. It is designed to prevent indefinite storage by default and to ensure that retention periods are linked to a concrete business or legal purpose.

## Scope
It applies to scan history, support correspondence, logs, analytics records, build artifacts, legal notices, and any records that may need to be retained for tax, audit, security, or regulatory reasons. The policy should distinguish operational retention from archival retention and should not treat them as the same thing.

## Operational Commitments
Retained data should be the minimum necessary for the stated purpose. When a record no longer serves a defined purpose, it should be deleted or de-identified rather than quietly kept. Archives should be access-controlled and should follow a stricter review path than live operational data.

## Application to Master QR
For Master QR, local scan history should not be retained longer than the user expects or longer than needed to support history features. If the app later adds cloud sync, retention and archiving rules will need to be revisited to avoid creating unnecessary historical logs.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed against actual storage behavior and should be tested with deletion workflows. Retention schedules should be documented and approved rather than improvised by individual teams.
