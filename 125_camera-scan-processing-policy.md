# Camera Scan Processing Policy

Master QR uses the camera exclusively for the functional purpose of decoding QR content. The application should process camera frames only for the immediate scan operation and should not transform those frames into a secondary surveillance or profiling channel. Any temporary frame handling must be strictly bounded to the scan session and discarded when the scan concludes. The policy requires contextual justification whenever the camera permission is requested, together with a clear explanation that the camera is necessary for barcode and QR recognition. No hidden background camera operation is permitted. If future enhancements introduce image upload, OCR, or cloud classification, those capabilities must be documented as separate processing activities, each with its own notice, consent treatment, and retention boundary.
