# Governing Law and Severability Clause

This clause anchors the legal framework that applies to the application’s terms and related notices. Master QR should state the governing law in a way that matches the developer’s chosen jurisdiction and the distribution model used across multiple storefronts, while also respecting non-waivable local consumer rights where applicable. The severability language should preserve the enforceability of the remaining terms if one provision becomes invalid or unenforceable. This clause is especially important for globally distributed software because a single product may be subject to multiple regulatory regimes at once. The legal drafting should therefore be precise, conservative, and compatible with the broader compliance documents bundled alongside the application.
