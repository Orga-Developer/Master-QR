# Device Compatibility Policy

Master QR is designed for a broad Android device surface, including devices with or without hardware cameras, varying screen sizes, and different runtime environments. Compatibility statements should reflect the actual operational boundaries of the app rather than promising universal support. The policy requires that camera-dependent functions fail gracefully when hardware is absent or inaccessible, and that the user be informed why certain capabilities cannot be used. Devices with restricted services, kiosk modes, foldable screens, or alternative store environments may encounter different feature availability. Compatibility testing should therefore be documented as part of release readiness, with explicit attention to permission prompts, storage behavior, ad rendering, and QR decoding performance across representative device classes.
