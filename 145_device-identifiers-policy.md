# Device Identifiers Policy

Any device identifiers processed by the application, including advertising IDs or vendor-specific signals, must be treated as potentially persistent and potentially regulated data. Master QR should disclose when such identifiers are used, why they are used, and how the user may reset or limit them through operating-system controls or consent settings. The policy should distinguish between identifiers required for basic security or service delivery and those used for analytics or monetization. Device identifiers may not be repurposed for undisclosed profiling or merged with unrelated records without a documented lawful basis. This clarity is essential for data-safety forms, app-store review, and the credibility of privacy notices presented within the application.
