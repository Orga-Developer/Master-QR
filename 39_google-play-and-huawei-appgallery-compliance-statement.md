## Purpose
This statement summarizes the product behaviors and operational commitments that matter to major Android distribution channels. Its role is to keep the app’s public legal posture synchronized with the requirements and review expectations of each store ecosystem.

## Scope
It applies to listing materials, store questionnaires, data-use disclosures, permission explanations, in-app legal links, and any requirements that differ by region or storefront. The statement should avoid promising compliance in the abstract and should instead describe the concrete controls the app team has implemented.

## Operational Commitments
Because store policies evolve, the statement should be framed as a living document rather than a one-time declaration. It should note where privacy policy links are published, how consent is surfaced, and which permission or data categories have been intentionally minimized to reduce review friction.

## Application to Master QR
Master QR’s current build contains camera access, internet access, advertising/analytics integrations, and a legal bundle for in-app viewing. That combination makes store compliance statements especially useful as a checkpoint before each release to major Android marketplaces.

## Review and Governance
The statement should be reviewed against the current published rules for the relevant storefronts before submission. Store-specific deltas should be captured in a change log.
