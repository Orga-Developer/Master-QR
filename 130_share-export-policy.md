# Share and Export Policy

Master QR includes export-oriented functionality such as sharing generated QR codes or copying decoded values. These interactions must be treated as intentional user transfers rather than passive background processing. The application should make it clear when data leaves the local device through a share sheet, clipboard, or external application handoff. Export formats should be kept minimal and human-readable, and they should avoid unnecessary metadata, identifiers, or hidden tracking parameters. If a user shares a QR image, the application should not append unsupported telemetry or embed undisclosed identifiers. This policy also requires that privacy-sensitive content receive an additional confirmation step before export, particularly where a user may inadvertently disclose credentials, tokens, or personal contact data.
