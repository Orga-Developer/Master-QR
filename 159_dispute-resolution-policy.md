# Dispute Resolution Policy

A dispute-resolution framework provides a predictable path for addressing disagreements about billing, support, policy interpretation, or legal notices. Even for a lightweight utility such as Master QR, users may raise complaints about refunds, consent handling, content restrictions, or account-like data deletion requests where applicable. The policy should define where claims are submitted, what evidence is required, and whether informal resolution is attempted before escalation. Where the law requires local consumer rights or alternative procedures, the policy should make clear that those rights are preserved. A well-written dispute policy reduces friction, improves support efficiency, and prevents small misunderstandings from becoming platform or regulatory problems.
