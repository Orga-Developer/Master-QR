# Minors and Age-Gating Policy

The application should provide a coherent position on minors because age-related rules can differ substantially across jurisdictions and storefronts. Master QR may choose to operate as a general audience utility while still restricting certain optional features, such as personalized advertising, where age thresholds trigger additional compliance duties. This policy requires that age gating, if implemented, be honest, minimally intrusive, and not used to collect more personal data than necessary. The wording should avoid false assurances and should make it clear that the product’s core QR functions are not designed to solicit sensitive information from minors. Any future family-related mode must be documented separately with its own data-use and parental-control framework.
