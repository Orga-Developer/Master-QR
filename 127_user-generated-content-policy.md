# User Generated Content Policy

Although Master QR is not a conventional social platform, users may create QR payloads, save text snippets, or export content that they generate themselves. Such material is treated as user-generated content for policy purposes. Users remain responsible for the legality, accuracy, and consequences of the data they encode into a QR code or share through the application. The app may provide tools to generate or store content, but it does not assume editorial responsibility for the underlying message. Prohibited content should include malware payloads, unlawful instructions, impersonation material, and content that attempts to evade safety controls. Moderation mechanisms, if ever added, must be described transparently so users understand whether content is reviewed, filtered, or reported.
