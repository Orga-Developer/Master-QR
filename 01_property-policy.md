# Property Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy establishes the principles governing ownership, stewardship, and permitted use of the application, its brand assets, source code, interface design, and related documentation. It clarifies that the software, visual identity, layout system, and derivative materials remain controlled by the rights holder unless an express written license says otherwise.

## Scope
It applies to all contributors, distributors, partners, translators, and service providers who interact with the project’s codebase, assets, or published materials. The policy also addresses internal handling of proprietary marks, documentation fragments, screen text, and build artifacts that may be redistributed across stores or mirrored in repositories.

## Operational Commitments
All use of protected assets must remain consistent with the project’s authorization structure. Any reproduction, modification, branding adaptation, or commercial redistribution must preserve attribution where required and must not create confusion about source, sponsorship, or endorsement. Unauthorized extraction of assets, packaging of proprietary materials, or misrepresentation of ownership is prohibited.

## Application to Master QR
The Master QR build contains app branding, iconography, interface copy, and store-facing materials that should be treated as controlled project assets. If the project is ever rebranded for regional store distribution, the ownership chain and allowed usage must be updated before launch.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed whenever the brand architecture, contributor model, or distribution strategy changes. Counsel should confirm that the policy matches any open-source components, third-party marks, and store-specific branding obligations.
