# Support and Complaints Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy defines how users may contact the operator for help, report problems, raise legal concerns, and submit complaints. It sets expectations about response channels, response quality, and the scope of issues that the support team is equipped to handle.

## Scope
It applies to email forms, web forms, in-app support pages, app-store messages, and any dedicated help address published in the product or its legal bundle. The policy should separate ordinary technical support from privacy, copyright, security, and regulatory complaints.

## Operational Commitments
Users should be able to report crashes, broken features, permission confusion, billing issues, and data-access concerns without having to guess the right department. The operator should also preserve a clear escalation path for unresolved complaints and for issues that require legal review.

## Application to Master QR
For Master QR, support should cover scanning failures, QR generation issues, local history behavior, consent problems, and store-installation questions. The support record should also be able to route privacy complaints to the same team that maintains the privacy policy and data deletion process.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed whenever contact details, service hours, or complaint-handling commitments change. A stable, monitored support address is essential for trust and store-review confidence.
