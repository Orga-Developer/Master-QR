# Software Architecture Overview

This document describes the architectural boundaries that govern the current build of Master QR. The application is intentionally lightweight and modular, centering on a camera-driven QR scanner, a QR generator, local scan history, language switching, consent handling, and ad-supported monetization. The architecture must preserve a strict separation between presentation logic, persistence, and platform services so that legal disclosures remain accurate and the product surface can be audited. Any future services, including analytics or cloud synchronization, should be introduced through explicit feature flags and documented data-flow reviews. The architecture is designed to support compliance by making every data pathway observable, minimal, and reversible, with local-first defaults and tightly bounded third-party integrations.
