# API and SDK Usage Policy

Master QR may rely on external APIs and SDKs only when their purpose is operationally necessary, contractually approved, and disclosed in the user-facing legal set. In the present build, the key integrations are Android camera APIs, Google Mobile Ads, Firebase initialization logic, and consent management services. Each integration must be configured according to least-privilege principles and only activated after the corresponding disclosure, consent, or policy notice has been satisfied. No SDK may be added to the application without a documented review of data collection, retention, export, subprocessors, and end-user impact. This policy applies equally to experimental builds, beta channels, and store-specific variants distributed under the com.qrcodereader.app namespace.
