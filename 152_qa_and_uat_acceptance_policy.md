# QA and UAT Acceptance Policy

Quality assurance and user acceptance testing must confirm not only technical correctness but also policy correctness. For Master QR, test cycles should verify camera permission timing, QR scan accuracy, consent behavior, language rendering, ad loading conditions, and local history persistence. Acceptance should also include a legal-content check, ensuring that privacy disclosures, terms, and help pages are internally consistent and reflect the active build. A test run is not complete until the reviewer confirms that no unintended data is collected, that the user can exit sensitive flows, and that the app does not misbehave when network access is unavailable. This policy makes QA part of the compliance system rather than a purely technical ritual.
