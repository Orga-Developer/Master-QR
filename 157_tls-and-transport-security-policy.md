# TLS and Transport Security Policy

All external communication should be protected with modern transport security controls, and the policy should clearly state that unsecured transport is not acceptable for sensitive or production traffic. Master QR should rely on TLS for any interaction with advertising, analytics, consent, or update services, and should avoid weak protocols or silent downgrade behavior. Certificate validation, hostname verification, and secure endpoint configuration are part of the trust model and must be reviewed before release. If the app supports pinned or restricted endpoints in the future, those controls should be documented to prevent confusion. This policy is especially important for cross-border deployments where intermediate network infrastructure may vary in quality or trustworthiness.
