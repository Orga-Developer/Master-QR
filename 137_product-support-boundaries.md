# Product Support Boundaries

A support policy must make it clear what the developer will and will not assist with. For Master QR, support should cover application defects, permission issues, store submission questions, and general troubleshooting for scanning, generation, and history features. Support should not extend to third-party QR content, device-level misconfiguration, or abuse of the app as a content distribution system. Because the product is distributed in multiple regions and stores, the support channel should define expected response windows, language scope, and escalation boundaries. This reduces user confusion and helps satisfy store-review expectations around contact availability. The policy should also explain how legal requests, privacy requests, and technical support are routed through different internal workflows.
