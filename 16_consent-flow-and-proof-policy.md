# Consent Flow and Proof Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy describes how the application obtains, records, refreshes, and proves user consent when consent is legally or contractually required. It exists to ensure that consent is not treated as a one-time banner but as a repeatable governance process with evidence.

## Scope
It applies to consent prompts, preference centers, audit logs, timestamps, device states, and any process used to demonstrate that a user accepted or rejected a particular choice. The policy should also address revocation, re-prompting after meaningful changes, and the retention of proof only for as long as necessary.

## Operational Commitments
Consent should be separated by purpose. Advertising consent, analytics consent, marketing consent, and permissions consent should not be merged into a single opaque acceptance path. Users should be able to understand what each choice does and to revise that choice without losing unrelated app functionality.

## Application to Master QR
Master QR’s ad-consent surface should be linked to the underlying SDK behavior and to any regional requirements that affect ad personalization. The proof record should be sufficient for internal audit without creating an unnecessary store of personal data.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed whenever the consent UI, the legal basis, or the underlying SDK logic changes. A mismatch between the prompt text and the actual code path must be corrected immediately.
