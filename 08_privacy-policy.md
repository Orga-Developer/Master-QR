## Purpose
This policy explains how personal and device-related information is collected, used, stored, shared, and protected in connection with the application. Its purpose is to provide a clear legal and operational account of privacy practices without obscuring the practical behavior of the product.

## Scope
It applies to store visitors, app users, support contacts, and any individual whose data may be processed by the app or its integrated service providers. The policy should cover identifiers, analytics events, device metadata, consent signals, support correspondence, and locally retained history where such information can identify a person or device.

## Operational Commitments
The policy should distinguish between data the app receives directly, data the device processes locally, and data that third-party SDKs may receive under their own terms. It should also explain user controls, lawful bases where applicable, retention logic, and the protection measures used to limit unauthorized access.

## Application to Master QR
Master QR appears to rely heavily on local processing, with scan history stored in Room and QR generation performed on-device. Because the build also contains advertising, analytics, and consent surfaces, the privacy policy should be explicit about what is local, what is shared with SDKs, and what is not transmitted to a server controlled by the app operator.

## Review and Governance
The privacy policy should be reviewed after every material feature change or SDK update. Any mismatch between actual code behavior and the policy language should be corrected before release.
