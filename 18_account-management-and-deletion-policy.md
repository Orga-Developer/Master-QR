# Account Management and Deletion Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy explains how accounts are created, maintained, accessed, corrected, and deleted where an application offers identity-based features. It is designed to make the lifecycle of account data transparent and to provide a predictable path for user requests.

## Scope
It applies to sign-up flows, login states, profile records, linked identifiers, support tickets, and any data that becomes associated with a named account. The policy should also address whether the app supports account-free use, guest mode, or device-only preferences.

## Operational Commitments
If account deletion is available, the policy should describe the in-app path, the web path, the categories of data removed, the categories retained for legal or anti-abuse reasons, and the approximate processing timeline. If no account exists, the document should say so plainly rather than implying a hidden service.

## Application to Master QR
Master QR currently appears to operate without a mandatory user account, relying instead on local history and device-side state. This policy therefore functions as a future-proof framework for any later cloud sync, premium login, or multi-device identity feature.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be re-reviewed before any account system is introduced. Deletion, portability, and retention logic should be tested end-to-end so the public promise matches the database behavior.
