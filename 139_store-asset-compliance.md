# Store Asset Compliance

Store assets include the app title, icons, screenshots, feature graphics, descriptions, privacy links, and support references submitted to distribution platforms. Master QR must ensure those assets do not overstate the product’s capabilities or imply functionality that is not present in the binary. Any depiction of QR scanning, ad behavior, consent prompts, or data handling must be truthful and not materially misleading. Store metadata should align with the privacy policy, data-safety disclosures, and permission usage notes, especially where camera access and internet connectivity are concerned. This policy also requires that promotional text avoid unsupported claims of security, anonymity, or fraud prevention unless those claims are independently substantiated and approved by compliance review.
