# COPPA Kids Safety Statement

Master QR is not designed as a children’s service, and this statement clarifies that the application is intended for a general audience rather than child-directed use. If a user is under the applicable age of digital consent in their jurisdiction, the appropriate guardian or legal representative should make the installation and consent decisions. The application should avoid child-directed advertising practices, manipulative flows, and data collection that would be inappropriate for a family-oriented environment. Where store categories or rating systems require age disclosures, the product should declare them accurately. This statement is important because QR tools can be deceptively simple and may otherwise be misclassified as child-facing despite the surrounding privacy and advertising obligations.
