# IP Address and Network Telemetry Policy

Network metadata such as IP address, request timing, and connectivity state may be incidentally processed whenever the app communicates with advertising, analytics, consent, or update services. This policy requires the developer to state that such information is typically used for security, diagnostics, fraud prevention, or service routing rather than for hidden behavioral profiling. Master QR should not claim that it never touches network metadata if third-party services are enabled. Instead, it should disclose the limited and incidental nature of the processing, the relevant retention window where known, and the user-facing settings that may reduce or disable non-essential traffic. Honest treatment of network telemetry is necessary for cross-border compliance and platform transparency.
