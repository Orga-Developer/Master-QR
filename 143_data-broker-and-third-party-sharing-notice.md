# Data Broker and Third-Party Sharing Notice

This notice explains that the application should not function as a data broker and should not sell user content in the traditional consumer-data brokerage sense. Nevertheless, the use of advertising networks, analytics services, crash-reporting tools, or consent-management SDKs may involve limited third-party sharing. Master QR must therefore describe any such sharing precisely and avoid ambiguous terms that suggest unrestricted disclosure. The notice should specify the categories of data shared, the contractual or operational purpose, and the user controls available to limit or reject such sharing. The goal is to distinguish essential technical exchanges from commercial resale, thereby preserving trust and ensuring that the app’s disclosures remain aligned with modern privacy expectations.
