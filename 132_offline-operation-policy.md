# Offline Operation Policy

Master QR should remain operational even when network connectivity is limited or unavailable, except where the user chooses features that inherently require online services such as advertising or remote telemetry. Offline-first behavior is a meaningful privacy and resilience characteristic because it reduces the amount of data sent off-device and strengthens availability in constrained environments. The policy requires that the app’s core scan and generation functions be usable without internet access where technically feasible. When a network dependency exists, the user-facing interface should identify that dependency instead of failing silently. This policy also supports store review by clarifying that connectivity is ancillary to the core utility of the product rather than a hidden prerequisite for basic use.
