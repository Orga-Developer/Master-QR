# Business Continuity Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy explains how the organization will continue to operate during disruptions that do not amount to a full disaster but still affect productivity, support, or compliance. Its purpose is to keep essential functions running while preserving security and accountability.

## Scope
It applies to staffing continuity, remote work readiness, access to legal documents, support response, release management, and critical vendor availability. The policy should identify manual fallbacks, alternates for key personnel, and communication channels that remain available during an outage.

## Operational Commitments
A continuity plan should preserve the ability to publish urgent notices, respond to user complaints, and manage security issues even when primary systems are impaired. Continuity is not only about infrastructure; it also concerns people, documents, and authority to make decisions.

## Application to Master QR
For Master QR, continuity planning should include the ability to update store listings, legal assets, and support pages if the main application pipeline is interrupted. The app’s offline orientation makes continuity simpler, but not optional.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed alongside staffing changes and vendor dependencies. A named continuity owner should verify readiness and keep the fallback procedures current.
