# Responsible Disclosure Policy

**Product:** Master QR  
**Package:** com.qrcodereader.app  
**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
This policy complements the vulnerability disclosure process by defining the conduct expected from anyone who discovers a weakness and wishes to share it responsibly. It emphasizes coordination, restraint, and a user-first remediation mindset.

## Scope
It applies to independent researchers, partners, employees, and consultants who may encounter a flaw while testing the application or its infrastructure. The policy should require that proof-of-concept activity remain minimal, that only the least amount of data necessary be accessed, and that the issue be reported privately.

## Operational Commitments
Responsible disclosure is not merely a reporting channel; it is a behavioral standard. The reporter should avoid persistence, lateral movement, public shaming, or attempts to monetize the weakness before the operator has a fair chance to investigate and patch it.

## Application to Master QR
For Master QR, any report involving code scanning, network requests, ad SDKs, or local history should be handled privately and with care. The app’s small data footprint does not eliminate the need for a disciplined and respectful escalation path.

## Review and Governance
The policy should be reviewed whenever the vulnerability process changes or when new systems are added to the security boundary. A consistent acknowledgement and triage flow helps preserve trust with the research community.
