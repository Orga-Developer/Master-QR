## Purpose
This policy governs the use of the Master QR name, logo, product identifiers, and any associated service marks. Its purpose is to prevent confusion, dilution, or improper co-branding while preserving the integrity of the application across stores, languages, and campaign materials.

## Scope
It applies to screenshots, metadata, icons, promotional pages, marketing releases, support materials, and partner listings. The policy also reaches variant spellings, translated names, domain references, and store badges that may imply affiliation with another entity or certification scheme.

## Operational Commitments
All trademark usage must be accurate, proportional, and approved. Third parties may not alter the mark, integrate it into their own brand identity, or use it in a way that suggests endorsement. When the mark is referenced in comparative or editorial contexts, the presentation must remain factual and non-misleading.

## Application to Master QR
Within the app and store listing, the trademark language should be consistent with the current package name, product title, and release channel. Any regional storefront or reseller that republishes the app must preserve the mark’s spelling, capitalization, and approved icon treatment.

## Review and Governance
