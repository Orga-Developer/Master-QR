# Crash Reporting and Error Logging Policy

Crash reports and error logs are valuable for quality assurance, but they can also contain sensitive fragments if not carefully constrained. Master QR should keep logging narrowly focused on technical diagnostics and avoid capturing full QR payloads, personal data, or unnecessary device identifiers unless such capture is indispensable to reproduce a defect. The policy should explain when crash reporting is active, whether it is tied to explicit consent, and how the user may disable non-essential diagnostics where the platform permits it. Logs retained for support or debugging must be access-controlled and time-bounded. This document therefore protects both engineering effectiveness and user privacy by making diagnostics transparent, limited, and revocable where possible.
